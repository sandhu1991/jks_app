Canadian Citizenship

Permanent residents of Canada can become Canadian citizens if they have been permanent residents of Canada and physically been in Canada for at least 3 of the last 5 years.

To become a Canadian citizen, most applicants must:
be a permanent resident.

be in good health.

have lived in Canada for at least 3 out of the last 5 years (1,095 days).

have filed your taxes, if you need to pass a citizenship test.

prove your language skills in English or French
